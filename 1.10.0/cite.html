A note on citing our work#

  • The main reference citation for DIPY is [GBA+14].

  • If you are using QuickBundles method please also cite [GBC+12].

  • If you are using track correspondence also cite [GBT+10].

  • If you are using Generalized Q-sampling please also cite [YWT10].

  • If you are using Free Water DTI, please also cite [NetoHenriquesRG+17].

  • If you are using Diffusional Kurtosis Imaging, please also cite Neto Henriques et al. [NetoHenriquesCM+21].

Citing other algorithms#

Depending on your research topic, it may also be appropriate to cite related method papers, some of which are listed in the documentation strings of the relevant functions or methods. All references cited in the DIPY codebase and documentation are collected in the General bibliography.
